About Me

Meet Varda - A super enthusiastic, creative hard-worker with a rich history and a life full of achievements.
Varda was born in the 1950s and grew up in Jerusalem.
From an early age, she showed immense dedication to her studies and passion for learning, excelling in various subjects.
She is known for her ability to build strong, lasting relationships and is deeply involved in her community and family life, constantly nurturing connections with friends, colleagues, and loved ones.
In her free time, Varda enjoys a wide range of hobbies, including getting lectures on cultures around the globe, watching foreign movies, hiking in nature, and practicing Pillates, which keeps her mobile and strong so she can be fit to maintain her enormous garden.
She's also an avid traveler, having explored many countries and cultures, each journey enriching her worldview.
Her love for adventure is balanced by her creativity in arts.
One of the most influential figures in Varda's life was her grandmother, a resilient and compassionate woman who taught her the value of hard work, empathy, and persistence.
Inspired by these values, Varda continues to strive for personal growth and enrich the lives of those around her.
As a pillar of her social and professional circles, Varda remains an inspiring role model, offering guidance and support to everyone in her network. Her life story is one of resilience, passion, and a constant drive to create meaningful connections and leave a positive impact on the world.
